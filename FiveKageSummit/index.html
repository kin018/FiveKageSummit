The Gathering

The 5 Kage Summit is an emergency meeting of all Kage as well as their bodyguards to discuss matters affecting their territory and state of the world. This years summit will take place in Tokyo, Japan instead of the Land of Iron due to safety concerns and will predominatley discuss matters pertaining to the Akatsuki.
